# BG3 Splendor Card Design Document

This document outlines the design philosophy, categorizations, and future expansion plans for the cards in BG3 Splendor.

## 1. Core Mechanics & The "Origin Companions" Plan
**IMPORTANT: The 7 Origin Companions (Shadowheart, Gale, Astarion, Lae'zel, Wyll, Karlach, Dark Urge) are EXCLUDED from the standard card market.**
- **Future Feature:** They will be introduced as a "Hero Selection" mechanic at the start of the game, giving each player a unique asymmetric passive skill or starting bonus.

## 2. Card Tier Segregation (Act-Based)
To create a narrative progression that matches a playthrough of *Baldur's Gate 3*, the three Splendor Tiers correspond directly to the three Acts of the game.

### Tier 1: Act 1 (The Wilderness, Underdark, Grymforge)
- **Quantity:** 30 Cards (6 per resource type)
- **Lore Focus:** Minor factions, local encounters, and early allies/traders.
- **Symmetric Cost Structures (6 variations per color):**
  1. 五种宝石各1个 (Cost: 1,1,1,1,1) -> 0 pts
  2. 三种宝石1个，一种宝石2个 (Cost: 2,1,1,1) -> 0 pts
  3. 两种宝石各2个 (Cost: 2,2) -> 0 pts
  4. 一种宝石3个，一种宝石1个 (Cost: 3,1) -> 0 pts
  5. 一种宝石3个 (Cost: 3) -> 0 pts
  6. 一种宝石4个 (Cost: 4) -> 1 pt

### Tier 2: Act 2 (The Shadow-Cursed Lands)
- **Quantity:** 20 Cards (4 per resource type)
- **Lore Focus:** The desperate struggle in the shadows. Key locations, crucial allies, and mid-game horrors.
- **Symmetric Cost Structures (4 variations per color):**
  1. 三种宝石分别3个、2个、2个 (Cost: 3,2,2) -> 1 pt
  2. 两种宝石分别4个、2个 (Cost: 4,2) -> 2 pts
  3. 一种宝石5个 (Cost: 5) -> 2 pts
  4. 一种宝石6个 (Cost: 6) -> 3 pts

### Tier 3: Act 3 (Baldur's Gate & The Endgame)
- **Quantity:** 15 Cards (3 per resource type)
- **Lore Focus:** Legendary dragons, High-tier artifacts, endgame bosses, and grand factions.
- **Symmetric Cost Structures (3 variations per color):**
  1. 一种宝石7个 (Cost: 7) -> 4 pts
  2. 三种宝石分别6个、3个、3个 (Cost: 6,3,3) -> 4 pts
  3. 两种宝石分别7个、3个 (Cost: 7,3) -> 5 pts

## 3. Patron (Noble) Design
- **Quantity:** 20 Patrons (3 are randomly selected per game).
- **Points:** Fixed at 3 points.
- **Requirement Profile:** Usually 8-9 total permanent gems across 2-3 specific colors.
- **Lore Focus:** Gods, Archdevils, and Overarching Factions that observe the mortal realm. Examples: The Dead Three (Bhaal, Myrkul, Bane), Archdevils (Asmodeus, Zariel, Raphael), and Deities (Mystra, Selûne, Shar).

## 4. Visual Aesthetics & Material Evolution
- **Tier 1:** Rough, tarnished iron and bronze borders. Simple and sturdy.
- **Tier 2:** Polished Mithril/Silver. Elegant geometric corner cuts, inner reflections.
- **Tier 3:** Heavy Gold/Brass. Ornate, non-rectangular borders with thorns, dragon bone motifs, and arcane floating elements extending past the frame.
