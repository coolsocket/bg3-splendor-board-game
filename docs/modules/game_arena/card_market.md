# Module: Card Market (卡牌市场)

The Card Market occupies the central area of the Game Arena. It displays available cards for purchase, organized by tiers.

## UI & Art (UI 与美术)

### Card Design
- **Material**: Cards have a rough paper or parchment texture.
- **Borders**: Unified borders with solid `border-[#bf953f]/40` for reserved slots, and specific tier-based styling if applicable.
- **Shadows**: Deep, offset `box-shadow` (`shadow-xl`) to make cards look like they are resting on the table.
- **Corners**: Rounded corners (`rounded-md`) with `overflow-hidden` to prevent background bleeding.

### Icons & Numbers
- **Prestige Points**: Displayed at the top-left of the card as a large number.
- **Production Bonus**: Displayed at the top-right as a rotated diamond (`rotate-45`) icon with the resource color/symbol.
- **Cost**: Displayed at the bottom as small colored circles with numbers.

## Interactions & Usage (交互与使用)

### Card States
- **Default**: Normal appearance.
- **Hover**: Card floats up slightly (`translate-y-[-4px]`), shadow becomes larger, and an arcane glow may appear if the card is affordable.
- **Affordable**: Highlighted with a soft glow or animation to indicate it can be bought.
- **Unavailable**: Dimmed or grayscaled if the player cannot afford it (though usually not strictly disabled in view, just not selectable for purchase).

### Actions
- **Select**: Clicking a card selects it, showing action buttons (Buy, Reserve).
- **Buy**: Deducts resources and adds the card to the player's board.
- **Reserve**: Adds the card to the player's reserved list.

### API Contract (Props)

#### `CardMarket`
| Prop | Type | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `onCardInteract` | `(action, cardId) => void` | Callback for buy/reserve/select. |

#### `Card`
| Prop | Type | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `id` | `string` | Unique card ID. |
| `tier` | `1 \| 2 \| 3` | Card tier. |
| `prestigePoints` | `number` | Victory points. |
| `providedBonus` | `ResourceType` | Asset generated by card. |
| `cost` | `Partial<Record<ResourceType, number>>` | Cost to purchase. |
| `isAffordable` | `boolean` | Controls glow effect. |
| `onInteract` | `(action, cardId) => void` | Click handler. |

### Store Context (外部状态依赖)
- **`usePublicStore`**: Reads `faceUpCards` and `decks` to display the market.
- **`usePlayerStore`**: Reads player assets to calculate `isAffordable` via domain rules.


### Future Abstractions
- The complex hover and selection logic in `Card` could be abstracted into a `SelectableCard` HOC or hook if needed for other card types.
