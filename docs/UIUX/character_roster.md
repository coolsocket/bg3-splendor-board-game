# BG3 Splendor Character & Patron Roster

This document details the complete roster of 65 standard market cards and 20 patrons, meticulously categorized by game acts (Tiers) and resource types, ensuring a deeply immersive Baldur's Gate 3 experience.

*Note: The 7 Origin Companions are excluded from this standard market and will be featured in a dedicated "Hero Selection" mechanic.*

## 🟢 Tier 1: Act 1 & 2 Minor Factions, NPCs, Encounters, and Locations (30 Cards)
**Cost Profile:** 3-5 total resources. **Points:** 0-1.

### ✨ Radiant
1. **Dammon**: The skilled tiefling hellrider smith.
2. **Zevlor**: Former Hellrider leading the refugees.
3. **Harper Stash**: Hidden supplies left by the Harpers.
4. **Barcus Wroot**: A determined Ironhand Gnome.
5. **Flaming Fist** (1 pt): Mercenaries enforcing the law of the Gate.
6. **Selûnite Outpost** (1 pt): An ancient temple warding off the Underdark.

### 🔮 Arcane
7. **Volo**: The famous bard and "expert" on everything.
8. **Alfira**: A sweet tiefling bard searching for a song.
9. **Omeluum**: A rogue mind flayer studying the arcane.
10. **Blurg**: Hobgoblin scholar of the Society of Brilliance.
11. **Iron Flask** (1 pt): Zhentarim cargo containing a dangerous beast.
12. **Arcane Tower** (1 pt): Lenore's isolated laboratory in the Underdark.

### 🌿 Nature
13. **Emerald Grove**: A sanctuary shielded by druidic magic.
14. **Scratch**: The best boy in all the Sword Coast.
15. **Owlbear Cub**: A fierce but adorable orphaned beast.
16. **Sovereign Spaw**: The peaceful leader of the Myconid Colony.
17. **Sussur Tree**: A massive anti-magic tree in the depths.
18. **Sovereign Glut** (1 pt): A deposed myconid seeking violent revenge.

### 🔥 Infernal
19. **Goblin Camp**: A raucous stronghold of the Absolute.
20. **Dror Ragzlin**: The hobgoblin warlord.
21. **Grymforge**: An ancient Sharran fortress over magma.
22. **Adamantine Forge**: A legendary forge capable of shaping mithral.
23. **Zhentarim Hideout**: A black market smuggling ring.
24. **Sazza** (1 pt): A captive goblin with information.

### 🌑 Dark
25. **Blighted Village**: An abandoned settlement overrun by goblins.
26. **Auntie Ethel**: A sweet old lady, or something far worse.
27. **Duergar Intruders**: Cruel slavers of the Underdark.
28. **Phase Spider Matriarch**: Queen of the Whispering Depths.
29. **True Soul Nere**: An arrogant drow trapped in a cave-in.
30. **Spectator** (1 pt): A lesser beholder guarding ancient secrets.

## 👑 Patrons (Nobles): 20 Entities
**Points:** Fixed at 3 points. **Requirements:** 8-9 total permanent gems across 2-3 specific colors.
*In each game, 3 Patrons are randomly selected to observe the mortal realm.*

### 💀 The Dead Three
1. **Bhaal**: The Lord of Murder. (Requires 4 Dark, 4 Infernal)
2. **Myrkul**: The Lord of Bones. (Requires 4 Dark, 4 Nature)
3. **Bane**: The Lord of Darkness. (Requires 4 Infernal, 4 Arcane)

### 🌙 The Twin Goddesses

5. **Shar**: The Nightsinger. (Requires 4 Dark, 4 Radiant)
